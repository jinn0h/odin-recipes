Tuna Kimchi Jjigae

Description

Kimchi-jjigae (김치찌개) or kimchi stew is a jjigae, or stew-like Korean dish, made with kimchi and other ingredients, such as pork or seafood, scallions, onions, and diced Dubu. It is one of the most common stews in Korean cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Aged Kimchi (Should taste slightly sour)
  • ¼ Whole Onion
  • 1 Tablespoon Gochujang Paste
  • 1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 1 Can of Tuna (~150 grams)
  • 2.5 Cups Water (~625 ml)
  • 1 Tablespoon Gochugaru Flakes
  • 1 Tablespoon Korean Anchovy Fish Sauce (or substitute with 1T of Soy Sauce)
  • ½ Tablespoon Minced Garlic
  • ½ Block Firm Tofu (~175 grams)

Steps

Prep Ingredients
  1. Chop some well-aged Kimchi. Cut enough to fill 1 cup - and set it aside for later. (Note: Pour any leftover kimchi juice on your cutting board into the cup as well).
  2. Dice ¼ a whole onion into thin strips.
  3. Cut a finger-length piece of spring onion stalk into thin circles.
Stir-Fry Kimchi
  1. Place the chopped Kimchi and sliced onions into a large pot or wok.
  2. Add-in Gochujang Paste (1 Tablespoon).
  3. Followed by Soy Sauce (1 Tablespoon).
  4. Then carefully open a can of tuna. Press the lid against the tuna and add-in only the oil at this point! (We'll add-in the actual tuna chunks at the end.)
  5. Place the pot on medium-high heat. Once it starts to sizzle, set a timer for 4 minutes! Stir-fry for 4 minutes - and remember to continuously stir (or else it will burn).
Putting everything together
  1. After 4 minutes of stir-frying, add in 2.5 cups (~625 ml) of water.
  2. Use a wooden spatula and scrape off any bits on the side of the pot into the soup.
  3. Now add-in Gochugaru Flakes (1 Tablespoon).
  4. Followed by Korean Anchovy Fish Sauce (1 Tablespoon). (Note: You can also substitute the Fish Sauce with 1T of Soy Sauce).
  5. Once it comes to a boil, add in Minced Garlic (½ Tablespoon).
  6. Then, add-in the by Sliced Chili Peppers as well as a few cracks of Black Pepper.
  7. Now let this soup boil away for 2 minutes!
  8. After 2 minutes, carefully add the sliced tofu pieces to one side of the pot.
  9. Finally scoop the tuna into the middle of the pot.
  10. Let everything boil away for a final 1 minute - all the flavors will come together!
  11. Bon Appetit - serve with a bowl of hot rice!
